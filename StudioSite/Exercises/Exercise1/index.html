Effervescent

For my Spring elective during my freshman year, I took Product, Promotion, and Packaging. We had four main projects, one of which being a cosmetic project where we were asked to create our own fragrance, which entailed coming up with our own branding identities, designing and printing our own packaging, and casting our own perfume bottles.

I centered my fragrance around the concept of a girl boss. Aimed at young adults, specifically aged 18-24, Effervescent offers a confidence sparkling from within.

Effervescent's brand identity is inspired by the word "effervescent" which means sparkling or bubbly.
